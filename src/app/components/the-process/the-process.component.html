The Process:

Gather Requirements: This is where the scope of your website
will be defined. During this phase, our main goal is to clearly define
the functionality and capabilities that your website will have.
This will help us determine which of our three web development
packages will work best for your situation. Website Development: The developers at HSV Web Dev, LLC will use
the information gathered in Step 1 to create an elegant website.
The website will be live within 1 week following our conclusion of Step 1.
We use the live website to gather immediate feedback from our customers.
For instance, we will create the layout of the website, call the customer, and instruct them to view
the website. The customer will be able to provide real time feedback throughout the development process.
This allows us to provide a superior website that is highly customized to
the needs of our customers. Continuous Improvement: This step never stops. We will make updates to your
website as needed. Our development process enables us to push out updates
as our clients need them.
